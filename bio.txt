Georgia Gkioxari is a research scientist at Facebook AI Research (FAIR). She
received a PhD in computer science and electrical engineering from the 
University of California at Berkeley under the supervision of Jitendra Malik 
in 2016. Her research interests lie in computer vision, with a focus on object 
and person recognition from static images and videos. In 2017, 
Georgia received the Marr Prize at ICCV for "Mask R-CNN".
